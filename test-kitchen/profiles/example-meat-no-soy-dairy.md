# Example: Meat Eater (No Soy / No Dairy)

## Restrictions
- No soy (no soy sauce, no tofu, no edamame, no miso)
- No dairy (no cheese, no butter, no yogurt, no cream, no milk)

## Preferences
- High protein
- Chicken, pork, and beef
- Likes bold, savory flavors

## Allergies
(none)

## Notes
- Use coconut aminos instead of soy sauce
- Use olive oil or coconut oil instead of butter
- Use lemon + olive oil + herbs instead of yogurt marinades
- Skip cheese/feta entirely — finish with fresh herbs, olives, or toasted nuts instead
