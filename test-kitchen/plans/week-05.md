# Meal Prep List — Week 5 (Simplified)

5 meals — 4 mains (veg + meat versions) + 1 breakfast.
**Meat eater:** No soy, no dairy.

| # | Dish | Vegetarian | Meat |
|---|------|-----------|------|
| 1 | Thai Massaman Curry | Tofu + chickpeas | Chicken thighs |
| 2 | Korean Bibimbap Bowls | Tofu | Beef (bulgogi-style) |
| 3 | West African Peanut Stew | Chickpeas + peanuts | Chicken thighs |
| 4 | Peruvian Ají de Gallina | Chickpeas + cannellini beans | Chicken thighs |
| 5 | Egg Muffins (breakfast) | Eggs + cheese | Eggs (no cheese) |

## 1. Thai Massaman Curry

**Base:** 2 tbsp massaman curry paste, 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk, 1 cup veggie broth, 1 lb baby potatoes (halved), 1 large carrot (roll-cut), 1 small onion (quartered), 2 tbsp peanut butter, 1 tbsp coconut aminos, 1 tbsp brown sugar, 1 tbsp tamarind paste. Serve over jasmine rice.

**Veg add:** 14 oz extra-firm tofu (cubed) + 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas. Use soy sauce instead of coconut aminos if desired.
**Meat add:** 1.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs (cut into pieces, sear first). Use coconut oil to sear.

**Method:** Sear protein in pot, remove. Fry curry paste 1 min, add coconut cream, then remaining coconut milk + broth + potatoes + carrot + onion. Simmer 15 min. Stir in peanut butter, coconut aminos, sugar, tamarind. Return protein, simmer 5 min. Serve over rice with crushed peanuts, cilantro, and lime.

## 2. Korean Bibimbap Bowls

**Base:** 2 cups spinach, 2 carrots (julienned), 1 zucchini (julienned), 8 oz mushrooms (sliced), 1 cup bean sprouts, 4 eggs (fried). Sauce: 3 tbsp gochujang, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tbsp coconut aminos, 1 tsp honey, 1 garlic clove. Serve over 3 cups short-grain rice.

**Veg add:** 14 oz extra-firm tofu (cubed, tossed in sauce, pan-fried crispy). Can use soy sauce in sauce.
**Meat add:** 1 lb thinly sliced beef (ribeye or sirloin). Bulgogi marinade: 3 tbsp coconut aminos, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tbsp honey, 3 garlic cloves, 1 tsp ginger, 1/2 Asian pear (grated). Marinate 30 min+.

**Method:** Sauté each veggie separately with a pinch of salt, ~3 min each. Cook protein (pan-fry tofu 8 min or sear beef 3 min/side over high heat). Fry eggs sunny-side up. Assemble bowls: rice, veggies, protein, egg, drizzle sauce.

## 3. West African Peanut Stew

**Base:** 2 tbsp oil, 1 large onion (diced), 4 garlic cloves, 1 tbsp ginger, 3 tbsp tomato paste, 1/2 cup peanut butter, 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes, 3 cups broth, 1 large sweet potato (cubed), 2 cups kale, 1 bell pepper (diced), 1 carrot (diced), 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/4 tsp cayenne, juice of 1 lime. Serve over rice.

**Veg add:** 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas + 1/2 cup roasted peanuts.
**Meat add:** 1.5 lbs bone-in chicken thighs (skin removed, brown first, shred later).

**Method:** Brown chicken if using, remove. Sauté onion 5 min, add garlic + ginger + tomato paste + spices 2 min. Stir in peanut butter, then tomatoes + broth. Add sweet potato, carrot, bell pepper (and chicken if using). Simmer covered 25–30 min. Add kale + chickpeas (veg), cook 5 min. Lime juice to finish. Serve over rice with crushed peanuts and cilantro.

---

## 4. Peruvian Ají de Gallina

**Base:** 2 tbsp oil, 1 large onion (diced), 4 garlic cloves, 2–3 tbsp ají amarillo paste, 1 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 cup walnuts (ground), 2 slices white bread (torn), 1.5 cups broth. Serve over rice with boiled yellow potatoes, black olives, and hard-boiled eggs.

**Veg add:** 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas + 1 can (15 oz) cannellini beans.
**Meat add:** 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs (poach 20 min, shred, reserve broth).

**Method:** Meat: poach chicken, shred, reserve 1.5 cups broth. Both: sauté onion 5 min, add garlic + ají amarillo + spices 2 min, add ground walnuts + bread 1 min. Add broth, simmer 10 min. Blend smooth with immersion blender. Add protein, simmer 5–10 min until sauce coats everything. Serve over rice with potatoes, olives, hard-boiled egg.

**Freezing:** Cool all dishes completely before portioning into airtight containers. Label with dish name and date. All mains freeze well for 2–3 months. Egg muffins freeze up to 3 months — reheat from frozen in microwave 60–90 seconds. Store rice separately from curries and stews. For bibimbap, freeze protein + veggies together but cook rice and fry eggs fresh.
