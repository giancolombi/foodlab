## Flavor Profiles
- Moroccan (warm spices, dried fruit)
- Cuban (cumin, oregano, citrus, olives)
- Ethiopian (berbere, lime)
- Greek/Mediterranean (herbs, tomato, olive oil)
- Mexican (cumin, salsa verde)

## Consolidated Shopping List

### Proteins
**Vegetarian:**
- 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas
- 2 cans (14 oz each) young jackfruit
- 2 cans (15 oz each) black beans
- 1 block (14 oz) extra-firm tofu
- 2 cans (15 oz each) butter beans
- 8 large eggs
- Feta cheese (for gigantes + burritos)
- Shredded cheddar/Mexican blend cheese (burritos)

**Meat (No Soy / No Dairy):**
- 6 bone-in chicken thighs (tagine)
- 1.5 lbs boneless chicken thighs (berbere)
- 2 lbs flank steak (ropa vieja)
- 12 oz pork sausage — chorizo or merguez, dairy-free casing
- Chorizo for burritos (dairy-free)

### Produce
- 3 large onions
- 2 heads garlic
- Fresh ginger (small knob)
- 3 bell peppers (red, yellow, orange)
- 1 large sweet potato
- 1 medium eggplant
- 1 large zucchini
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 8 oz mushrooms (x2 — berbere + burritos)
- 2 cups fresh spinach
- 1 bunch kale or collard greens (if adding to ropa vieja)
- 3 large potatoes (gigantes)
- 2 carrots
- 2 ribs celery
- 3 large tomatoes
- 1 cup diced tomatoes (burritos)
- 1 lime
- 1 lemon
- Fresh parsley (1 bunch)
- Fresh cilantro (1 bunch)
- Fresh dill (for tahini dressing)

### Canned / Jarred
- 1 can (28 oz) whole peeled tomatoes
- 2 cans (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- Tomato paste (small can)
- Pimento-stuffed green olives (small jar)
- Preserved lemon (or extra lemons)
- Salsa verde (burritos)

### Dried Fruit
- Dried apricots (1/4 cup)
- Dried figs (1/4 cup)

### Pantry / Spices
- Cumin
- Coriander
- Cinnamon
- Allspice
- Ginger (ground)
- Cloves
- Saffron (optional)
- Oregano
- Berbere spice blend
- Paprika
- Dried thyme
- Garlic powder
- Onion flakes
- Red pepper flakes
- Bay leaves
- Tahini
- Pine nuts
- Couscous
- White rice
- White wine (1/2 cup, or extra broth)
- Coconut aminos (meat version soy sauce sub)
- 8 Siete tortillas

### Broth
- Vegetable broth (for veg versions)
- Chicken broth (for meat versions)

## Notes
- All mains freeze well
- Each dish shares the same base between veg and meat — only protein swaps
- Meat versions are 100% soy-free and dairy-free
