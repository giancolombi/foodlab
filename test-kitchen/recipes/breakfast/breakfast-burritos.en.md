# Breakfast Burritos

**Freezer-friendly:** Yes | **Prep:** 10 min | **Cook:** 15 min | **Makes:** ~8 burritos

## Ingredients
- 8 Siete tortillas (grain-free)
- 8 large eggs, scrambled
- 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and warmed
- 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
- 2 bell peppers, diced
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 cups fresh spinach
- 8 oz mushrooms, diced
- 1 cup diced tomatoes
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt, pepper, cumin to taste
- Salsa verde on the side

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Sauté onion, peppers, and mushrooms until soft, ~5 min.
2. Add spinach and tomatoes, cook until spinach wilts, ~2 min.
3. Push veggies to one side, scramble eggs on the other side. Mix together.
4. Warm tortillas briefly in microwave or dry skillet.
5. Assemble: eggs + veggies, black beans, cheese. Roll tightly, tucking in ends.

## Freezing
- Wrap each burrito tightly in foil.
- Place in freezer bag, squeeze out air.
- Freeze up to 3 months.
- Reheat: unwrap foil, wrap in damp paper towel, microwave 2–3 min, flipping halfway.

---

*Note: This is the vegetarian version. Meat eater can add cooked chorizo and skip the cheese.*
