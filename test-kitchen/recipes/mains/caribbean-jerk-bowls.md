# Caribbean Jerk Bowls

**Cuisine:** Jamaican / Caribbean | **Freezer-friendly:** Yes (freeze protein + rice separately) | **Prep:** 15 min + marinate | **Cook:** 30 min

## Jerk Marinade / Spice Rub (both versions)
- 1 tbsp allspice (pimento)
- 1 tbsp dried thyme
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp cayenne (or 1 scotch bonnet, minced — adjust heat)
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp nutmeg
- 3 scallions, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Juice of 2 limes
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- Salt to taste

Blend or mix everything into a paste.

## Coconut Rice & Beans (both versions — naturally dairy-free and soy-free)
- 1.5 cups long-grain rice
- 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
- 1 cup water
- 1 can (15 oz) red kidney beans, drained
- 2 cloves garlic, smashed
- 2 sprigs fresh thyme
- Salt to taste

Combine all in a pot. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, simmer 18 min. Fluff with fork.

## Toppings
- 1 mango, diced
- 1 avocado, sliced
- Quick-pickled red onion
- Fresh cilantro
- Lime wedges

---

## Vegetarian Version
**Protein:** 1 can (15 oz) red kidney beans (extra, beyond rice) + 1 block (14 oz) extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed

1. Toss tofu cubes in jerk marinade. Marinate at least 30 min (or overnight).
2. Preheat oven to 400°F. Spread tofu on a parchment-lined sheet pan.
3. Bake 25–30 min, flipping halfway, until crispy and caramelized.
4. Make coconut rice & beans while tofu bakes.
5. Assemble bowls: rice & beans, jerk tofu, mango, avocado, pickled onion, cilantro, lime.

---

## Meat Version (No Soy / No Dairy)
**Protein:** 2 lbs bone-in chicken thighs (or 1.5 lbs pork tenderloin, sliced)

1. Coat chicken in jerk marinade. Marinate at least 1 hour (overnight is best).
2. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place chicken skin-side up on a sheet pan.
3. Roast 30–35 min until chicken reaches 165°F and skin is charred.
4. Make coconut rice & beans while chicken roasts.
5. Assemble bowls: rice & beans, jerk chicken, mango, avocado, pickled onion, cilantro, lime.

## Freezing
- Freeze jerk protein in marinade (raw) for up to 6 weeks — marinating stops when frozen.
- Freeze cooked rice & beans separately for up to 3 months.
- Thaw overnight in fridge, reheat protein in oven at 375°F.
