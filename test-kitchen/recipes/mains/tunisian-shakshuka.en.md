# Tunisian Shakshuka

**Cuisine:** Tunisian / North African | **Freezer-friendly:** Yes — freeze the sauce base (up to 3 months); add eggs fresh when reheating | **Prep:** 10 min | **Cook:** 25 min

## Shared Base
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 large onion, diced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 green bell pepper, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp chili flakes (adjust to taste)
- 1 tsp sugar (optional, balances acidity)
- 2 cups fresh spinach or kale, roughly chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish

## Serve With
- Warm pita bread or crusty sourdough
- Cooked couscous or rice for a heartier meal

---

## Vegetarian Version
**Protein:** 6 eggs + 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained + 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

1. Heat olive oil in a large deep skillet over medium heat. Sauté onion and bell peppers until soft, ~7 min.
2. Add garlic, cumin, paprika, and chili flakes. Cook 1 min until fragrant.
3. Stir in tomato paste, then pour in crushed tomatoes. Add sugar if using. Stir well.
4. Simmer uncovered 10 min until the sauce thickens slightly.
5. Stir in spinach and chickpeas. Cook 2 min until spinach wilts.
6. Make 6 wells in the sauce with the back of a spoon. Crack an egg into each well.
7. Cover the skillet and cook on medium-low for 5–7 min until egg whites are set but yolks are still runny.
8. Crumble feta over the top. Garnish with cilantro.
9. Serve straight from the skillet with warm pita.

---

## Meat Version (No Soy / No Dairy)
**Protein:** 6 eggs + 1 lb ground beef or diced chicken breast

1. Heat olive oil in a large deep skillet over medium-high heat. Brown the ground beef (or sear diced chicken), breaking it up, ~5 min. Remove and set aside.
2. In the same skillet, sauté onion and bell peppers until soft, ~7 min.
3. Add garlic, cumin, paprika, and chili flakes. Cook 1 min until fragrant.
4. Stir in tomato paste, then pour in crushed tomatoes. Add sugar if using. Stir well.
5. Return the browned meat to the skillet. Simmer uncovered 10 min until sauce thickens.
6. Stir in spinach. Cook 2 min until wilted.
7. Make 6 wells in the sauce. Crack an egg into each well.
8. Cover and cook on medium-low for 5–7 min until egg whites are set but yolks are still runny.
9. Garnish with cilantro. Serve straight from the skillet with warm pita.
