# Filipino Adobo

**Cuisine:** Filipino | **Freezer-friendly:** Yes (up to 3 months) | **Prep:** 10 min | **Cook:** 40 min

## Shared Base
- 1 tbsp olive oil (or neutral oil)
- 1 large onion, sliced into half-moons
- 8 cloves garlic, smashed and roughly chopped
- 1/2 cup white vinegar (or cane vinegar)
- 1 cup broth (chicken or vegetable)
- 3 bay leaves
- 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
- 1 large potato, cubed (or 1 cup cubed kabocha squash)
- 2 cups green beans, trimmed and halved
- 1 cup spinach or bok choy, roughly chopped
- Salt to taste
- White rice for serving

## Serve With
- Steamed white rice
- Sliced scallions and a drizzle of sauce from the pot

---

## Vegetarian Version
**Protein:** 1 block (14 oz) extra-firm tofu, cubed + 1 can (15 oz) white beans (cannellini), drained
**Sauce:** 1/4 cup soy sauce + 1 tbsp brown sugar

1. Press tofu for 10 min, then cut into 1-inch cubes.
2. Heat oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Sear tofu until golden on all sides, ~6 min. Remove and set aside.
3. In the same pot, sauté onion until softened, ~4 min. Add garlic, cook 1 min.
4. Pour in vinegar, soy sauce, broth, and brown sugar. Add bay leaves and peppercorns. Stir to combine.
5. Add potatoes. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cover and cook 20 min until potatoes are tender.
6. Add green beans, white beans, and seared tofu. Simmer uncovered 10 min.
7. Add spinach, cook 2 min until wilted.
8. Remove bay leaves. Taste and adjust salt.
9. Serve over steamed rice, spooning extra sauce from the pot over everything. Top with scallions.

---

## Meat Version (No Soy / No Dairy)
**Protein:** 2 lbs bone-in chicken thighs, skin removed
**Sauce:** 1/4 cup coconut aminos + 1 tbsp brown sugar

1. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
2. Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Brown chicken on both sides, ~4 min per side. Remove and set aside.
3. In the same pot, sauté onion until softened, ~4 min. Add garlic, cook 1 min.
4. Pour in vinegar, coconut aminos, broth, and brown sugar. Add bay leaves and peppercorns. Stir to combine.
5. Return chicken to the pot. Add potatoes. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
6. Cover and cook 25 min until chicken is cooked through (165 F internal) and potatoes are tender.
7. Remove chicken, shred with two forks, discard bones. Return meat to pot.
8. Add green beans. Simmer uncovered 8 min until beans are tender and sauce has reduced.
9. Add spinach, cook 2 min until wilted. Remove bay leaves.
10. Taste and adjust salt. Serve over steamed rice, spooning sauce over everything. Top with scallions.
