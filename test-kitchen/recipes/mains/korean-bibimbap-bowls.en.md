# Korean Bibimbap Bowls

**Cuisine:** Korean | **Freezer-friendly:** Partially (freeze protein + veggies; cook rice fresh) | **Prep:** 20 min | **Cook:** 25 min

## Shared Bibimbap Sauce
- 3 tbsp gochujang (Korean chili paste — check label: most are soy-free, some aren't)
- 1 tbsp sesame oil (sesame is NOT soy — OK for both versions)
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp coconut aminos (or soy sauce for veg version)
- 1 tsp honey or brown sugar
- 1 clove garlic, minced

Mix all together. Set aside.

## Shared Vegetables (sauté each separately in a little oil, season with salt)
- 2 cups fresh spinach, wilted
- 2 carrots, julienned
- 1 zucchini, julienned
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 1 cup bean sprouts
- 4 eggs (fried, one per bowl)

## Base
- 3 cups cooked short-grain rice

---

## Vegetarian Version
**Protein:** 1 block (14 oz) extra-firm tofu, cubed

1. Press tofu 15 min. Cube and toss with 2 tbsp bibimbap sauce.
2. Pan-fry tofu in a hot skillet with oil until crispy on all sides, ~8 min. Set aside.
3. Sauté each vegetable separately (spinach, carrots, zucchini, mushrooms, bean sprouts) with a pinch of salt. ~3 min each.
4. Fry eggs sunny-side up.
5. Assemble bowls: rice on bottom, arrange veggies and tofu in sections on top.
6. Top with fried egg and drizzle bibimbap sauce.
7. Mix everything together before eating.

---

## Meat Version (No Soy / No Dairy)
**Protein:** 1 lb thinly sliced beef (ribeye or sirloin) — bulgogi-style

**Bulgogi Marinade (soy-free):**
- 3 tbsp coconut aminos
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ginger, grated
- 1/2 Asian pear, grated (or 1 tbsp apple sauce)
- Black pepper to taste

1. Marinate beef at least 30 min (or overnight).
2. Cook beef in a hot skillet over high heat, ~3 min per side until caramelized.
3. Sauté each vegetable separately with salt. ~3 min each.
4. Fry eggs sunny-side up.
5. Assemble bowls: rice, veggies, beef in sections.
6. Top with fried egg and bibimbap sauce (use coconut aminos version).

## Meal Prep Notes
- Store veggies and protein in containers, rice separately.
- Fry eggs fresh when eating.
- Keeps 4 days in fridge. Protein + veggies freeze well up to 2 months.
