# Moroccan Tagine

**Cuisine:** Moroccan | **Freezer-friendly:** Yes | **Prep:** 20 min | **Cook:** 1.5 hrs

## Shared Spice Base
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp ginger
- 1/4 tsp allspice
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- Pinch of cloves
- Pinch of saffron (optional)
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 large onion, diced
- Salt to taste

## Shared Ingredients
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 large sweet potato, cubed
- 1 medium eggplant, cubed
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- 1/4 cup dried apricots, halved
- 1/4 cup dried figs, halved
- 1 preserved lemon, chopped (or zest + juice of 1 lemon)
- Couscous for serving

## Garnish
- Toasted pine nuts
- Fresh parsley

---

## Vegetarian Version
**Protein:** 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained

1. Heat olive oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Sauté onion until soft, ~5 min.
2. Add garlic and all spices, cook 1 min until fragrant.
3. Add sweet potato, eggplant, tomatoes, apricots, figs, and preserved lemon.
4. Add 1 cup vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer.
5. Cover and cook on low 40–50 min until sweet potato is tender.
6. Add chickpeas, cook 10 more min.
7. Serve over couscous, garnish with pine nuts and parsley.

---

## Meat Version (No Soy / No Dairy)
**Protein:** 6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs

1. Season chicken thighs with the spice mix and salt.
2. Heat olive oil in a Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Sear chicken skin-side down until golden, ~5 min per side. Remove and set aside.
3. In the same pot, sauté onion until soft, ~5 min.
4. Add garlic and remaining spices, cook 1 min.
5. Add sweet potato, eggplant, tomatoes, apricots, figs, preserved lemon, and 1 cup chicken broth.
6. Nestle chicken thighs on top, skin-side up.
7. Cover, bake at 350°F for 1 hour until chicken is cooked through and sweet potato is tender.
8. Serve over couscous, garnish with pine nuts and parsley.
