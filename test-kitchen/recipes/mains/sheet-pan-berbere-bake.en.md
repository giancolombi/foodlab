# Sheet-Pan Berbere Bake

**Cuisine:** Ethiopian-inspired | **Freezer-friendly:** Yes | **Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 30 min

## Shared Spice Rub
- 1 tbsp berbere spice
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp dried thyme
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion flakes
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Juice of 1 lime

## Shared Vegetables
- 8 oz mushrooms, quartered
- 1 large zucchini, cubed
- 2 bell peppers, chunked
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 can (15 oz) white beans, drained

## Tahini Dressing
- 3 tbsp tahini
- 3 tbsp warm water
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/4 tsp dill
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- Pinch of salt

Whisk all dressing ingredients until smooth. Add more water if needed.

---

## Vegetarian Version
**Protein:** 1 block (14 oz) extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed

1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a large sheet pan with parchment.
2. Mix spice rub (berbere through lime juice) in a large bowl.
3. Toss tofu, mushrooms, zucchini, peppers, broccoli, and white beans in the spice mixture.
4. Spread in a single layer on the sheet pan.
5. Roast 25–30 min, flipping halfway, until veggies are charred at edges and tofu is crispy.
6. Drizzle with tahini dressing. Serve with rice or flatbread.

---

## Meat Version (No Soy / No Dairy)
**Protein:** 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cubed

1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a large sheet pan with parchment.
2. Mix spice rub in a large bowl.
3. Toss chicken, mushrooms, zucchini, peppers, broccoli, and white beans in the spice mixture.
4. Spread in a single layer on the sheet pan.
5. Roast 25–30 min, flipping halfway, until chicken reaches 165°F and veggies are charred.
6. Drizzle with tahini dressing. Serve with rice or flatbread.
