# Georgian Chicken Stew (Chakhokhbili)

**Cuisine:** Georgian | **Freezer-friendly:** Yes (up to 3 months) | **Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 45 min

## Shared Base
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 large onions, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
- 2 bell peppers (red or green), diced
- 1 large carrot, diced
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp ground fenugreek (or extra coriander if unavailable)
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 cup fresh cilantro, roughly chopped (divided — half for cooking, half for garnish)
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Juice of 1 lemon

## Serve With
- White rice, crusty bread, or flatbread
- Extra fresh cilantro and a lemon wedge

---

## Vegetarian Version
**Protein:** 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained + 1 block (14 oz) extra-firm tofu, cubed

1. Press tofu for 10 min, then cut into 1-inch cubes. Season with salt, coriander, and paprika.
2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Sear tofu cubes until golden on all sides, ~6 min. Remove and set aside.
3. Add remaining oil. Sauté onions until soft and translucent, ~5 min.
4. Add garlic, cook 1 min. Stir in coriander, paprika, fenugreek, and red pepper flakes. Cook 1 min.
5. Add crushed tomatoes, broth, bell peppers, and carrot. Drop in bay leaves and half the cilantro.
6. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cover and cook 25 min until veggies are tender.
7. Add chickpeas and seared tofu. Simmer uncovered 10 min to thicken.
8. Remove bay leaves. Stir in lemon juice, parsley, and remaining cilantro.
9. Season with salt and pepper. Serve over rice or with bread.

---

## Meat Version (No Soy / No Dairy)
**Protein:** 2 lbs bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs

1. Season chicken thighs generously with salt, pepper, coriander, and paprika.
2. Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Brown chicken skin-side down, ~5 min per side. Remove and set aside.
3. In the same pot, sauté onions in the rendered fat until soft, ~5 min, scraping up browned bits.
4. Add garlic, cook 1 min. Stir in coriander, paprika, fenugreek, and red pepper flakes. Cook 1 min.
5. Add crushed tomatoes, broth, bell peppers, and carrot. Drop in bay leaves and half the cilantro.
6. Return chicken to the pot, nestling it into the sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
7. Cover and cook 30 min until chicken is cooked through (165 F internal).
8. Remove chicken, shred off the bone with two forks, discard bones and skin. Return meat to pot.
9. Simmer uncovered 5 min to thicken. Remove bay leaves.
10. Stir in lemon juice, parsley, and remaining cilantro. Season with salt and pepper.
11. Serve over rice or with crusty bread.
