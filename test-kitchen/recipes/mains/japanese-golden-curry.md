# Japanese Golden Curry

**Cuisine:** Japanese | **Freezer-friendly:** Yes (freeze without rice) | **Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 45 min

## Homemade Curry Roux (No Soy / No Dairy)
- 3 tbsp coconut oil (or olive oil)
- 3 tbsp flour
- 2 tbsp curry powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Melt oil in a small pan over low heat. Whisk in flour, cook 2 min. Add curry powder, garam masala, turmeric, cinnamon. Stir until a thick paste forms, ~3 min. Set aside.

## Shared Base
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 2 large potatoes, cubed
- 2 carrots, sliced into rounds
- 1 cup mushrooms, quartered
- 1/2 cup frozen peas
- 3.5 cups broth (vegetable or chicken)
- 1 tbsp ketchup (for sweetness)
- 1 tbsp coconut aminos
- 1 tsp honey or brown sugar
- Salt to taste

## Serve With
- Steamed short-grain rice
- Pickled ginger (optional)

---

## Vegetarian Version
**Protein:** 1 block (14 oz) extra-firm tofu, cubed and pan-fried + 1 can (15 oz) white beans

1. Pan-fry tofu cubes in oil until golden on all sides. Set aside.
2. In a large pot, heat coconut oil. Sauté onion until deeply golden, ~10 min (this sweetness is key to Japanese curry).
3. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 min.
4. Add potatoes, carrots, mushrooms. Stir 2 min.
5. Pour in vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then simmer 20 min until potatoes are tender.
6. Stir in curry roux, ketchup, coconut aminos, and honey. Simmer 10 min until thick.
7. Add white beans, peas, and pan-fried tofu. Cook 5 min.
8. Serve over rice.

---

## Meat Version (No Soy / No Dairy)
**Protein:** 1.5 lbs boneless chicken thighs (or pork shoulder), cubed

1. Season meat with salt and curry powder.
2. In a large pot, heat coconut oil over medium-high. Brown meat on all sides, ~5 min. Remove.
3. Sauté onion until deeply golden, ~10 min.
4. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 min.
5. Add potatoes, carrots, mushrooms. Stir 2 min.
6. Pour in chicken broth. Return meat to pot. Bring to a boil, reduce to simmer.
7. Cover and cook 25 min until potatoes and meat are tender.
8. Stir in curry roux, ketchup, coconut aminos, honey. Simmer 10 min until thick.
9. Add peas, cook 3 min. Serve over rice.
