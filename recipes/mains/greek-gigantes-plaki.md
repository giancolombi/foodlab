# Greek Gigantes Plaki (Baked Beans)

**Cuisine:** Greek / Mediterranean | **Freezer-friendly:** Yes | **Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 30 min

## Shared Base
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 large onion, diced
- 1 carrot, diced
- 2 ribs celery, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- Pinch of cinnamon
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 cans (15 oz each) butter beans (gigantes), drained
- Salt to taste

## Serve With
- Crusty bread
- Green salad

---

## Vegetarian Version
**Finish:** Crumbled feta + drizzle of olive oil

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Heat olive oil in an oven-safe Dutch oven or baking dish over medium heat. Sauté onion, carrot, and celery until soft, ~7 min.
3. Add garlic, cook 1 min. Add tomatoes, herbs, spices, and bay leaf.
4. Simmer 5 min. Stir in butter beans.
5. Transfer to oven (uncovered). Bake 20–30 min until bubbly and slightly thickened.
6. Remove bay leaf. Top with crumbled feta, drizzle olive oil, sprinkle parsley.

---

## Meat Version (No Soy / No Dairy)
**Protein:** 12 oz pork sausage (chorizo, Italian, or merguez — check for dairy-free casing), sliced into rounds

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Heat olive oil in an oven-safe Dutch oven. Brown sausage rounds on both sides, ~4 min. Remove and set aside.
3. In the same pot, sauté onion, carrot, and celery until soft, ~7 min.
4. Add garlic, cook 1 min. Add tomatoes, herbs, spices, and bay leaf.
5. Simmer 5 min. Stir in butter beans and browned sausage.
6. Transfer to oven (uncovered). Bake 20–30 min until bubbly.
7. Remove bay leaf. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle parsley. No feta.
