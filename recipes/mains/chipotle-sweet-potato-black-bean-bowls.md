# Chipotle Sweet Potato & Black Bean Bowls

**Cuisine:** Mexican-inspired | **Freezer-friendly:** Partially (freeze protein + beans + sweet potatoes; cook rice fresh) | **Prep:** 15 min | **Cook:** 30 min

## Shared Roasted Sweet Potatoes
- 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed (about 1 inch)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Shared Spiced Black Beans
- 2 cans (15 oz each) black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp chipotle chili powder (or 1 minced chipotle in adobo)
- Salt to taste

## Shared Bowl Base
- 3 cups cooked brown rice or cilantro-lime rice
- 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or charred)
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 cup shredded red cabbage
- 1 avocado, sliced
- Fresh cilantro, for garnish
- Lime wedges

## Chipotle-Lime Drizzle (dairy-free)
- 3 tbsp tahini (or cashew butter)
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tsp adobo sauce (from canned chipotles)
- 1 small clove garlic, minced
- 2-3 tbsp warm water, to thin
- Pinch of salt

Whisk all drizzle ingredients until smooth. Adjust water for desired consistency.

---

## Vegetarian Version
**Protein:** 1 block (14 oz) extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed

1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a large sheet pan with parchment.
2. Toss sweet potato cubes with olive oil, cumin, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Spread on one half of the sheet pan.
3. Toss tofu cubes with 1 tbsp olive oil, 1/2 tsp cumin, and a pinch of chipotle chili powder. Spread on the other half.
4. Roast 25-30 min, flipping halfway, until sweet potatoes are tender and tofu is crispy.
5. While roasting, warm black beans in a small pot with cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, chipotle, and salt. Simmer 5 min.
6. Assemble bowls: rice on the bottom, then sweet potatoes, black beans, tofu, corn, bell pepper, and cabbage.
7. Top with avocado, drizzle with chipotle-lime sauce, garnish with cilantro and a lime wedge.

---

## Meat Version (No Soy / No Dairy)
**Protein:** 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast, cubed

**Chipotle Chicken Seasoning:**
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp chipotle chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste

1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a large sheet pan with parchment.
2. Toss sweet potato cubes with olive oil, cumin, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Spread on one half of the sheet pan.
3. Toss chicken cubes with olive oil and chipotle chicken seasoning. Spread on the other half.
4. Roast 25-30 min, flipping halfway, until sweet potatoes are tender and chicken reaches 165°F.
5. While roasting, warm black beans in a small pot with cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, chipotle, and salt. Simmer 5 min.
6. Assemble bowls: rice on the bottom, then sweet potatoes, black beans, chicken, corn, bell pepper, and cabbage.
7. Top with avocado, drizzle with chipotle-lime sauce, garnish with cilantro and a lime wedge.

## Meal Prep Notes
- Sweet potatoes, beans, and protein store well for 4-5 days in the fridge.
- Freeze protein + beans + sweet potatoes together for up to 2 months. Thaw overnight.
- Slice avocado and prep fresh toppings day-of for best texture.
- Cook rice fresh or store separately to avoid sogginess.
