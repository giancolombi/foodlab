# Lentil Bolognese

**Cuisine:** Italian | **Freezer-friendly:** Yes | **Prep:** 10 min | **Cook:** 40 min

## Shared Base
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 large onion, finely diced
- 3 carrots, finely diced
- 3 ribs celery, finely diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup vegetable broth
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Vegetarian Version
**Protein:** 1.5 cups dried green or brown lentils + 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

1. Heat oil in a large pot. Sauté onion, carrots, celery until soft, ~8 min.
2. Add garlic and tomato paste, cook 2 min.
3. Add lentils, crushed tomatoes, broth, and herbs.
4. Bring to a boil, reduce to simmer. Cover 30 min until lentils are tender.
5. Stir in walnuts. Adjust seasoning.
6. Serve over pasta or zoodles.

**Veggies to hide:** Finely diced zucchini, mushrooms, bell peppers, spinach (wilt in at end).
