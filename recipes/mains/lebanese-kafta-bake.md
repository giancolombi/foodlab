# Lebanese Kafta bil Sayniyeh (Baked Kafta Tray)

**Cuisine:** Lebanese / Levantine | **Freezer-friendly:** Yes | **Prep:** 20 min | **Cook:** 1 hr

## Shared Spice Mix
- 1 tsp allspice
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp chili flakes
- Salt to taste

## Shared Tray Ingredients
- 3 large potatoes, sliced into 1/4" rounds
- 3 large tomatoes, sliced into rounds
- 1 large onion, thinly sliced into rings
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped

## Tahini Sauce (both versions)
- 3 tbsp tahini
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp warm water
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- Pinch of salt

Whisk until smooth.

## Serve With
- Lebanese rice (rice with vermicelli) or pita bread
- Simple salad

---

## Vegetarian Version
**Protein:** Lentil-walnut kafta patties

**Lentil Kafta:**
- 1.5 cups cooked green or brown lentils
- 1/2 cup walnuts, finely chopped
- 1/2 onion, finely minced
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley, minced
- Spice mix (above)
- 2 tbsp breadcrumbs (to bind)

1. Mash lentils roughly (leave some texture). Mix with walnuts, onion, parsley, spices, breadcrumbs.
2. Form into small oval patties, ~2" each.
3. Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly oil a large baking tray.
4. Layer: potato rounds on bottom, drizzle with oil, season with salt. Add kafta patties. Top with tomato slices and onion rings.
5. Cover with foil. Bake 40 min.
6. Remove foil, bake 15–20 more min until potatoes are golden and tomatoes are caramelized.
7. Drizzle with tahini sauce and sprinkle parsley.

---

## Meat Version (No Soy / No Dairy)
**Protein:** Ground beef (or lamb) kafta

**Beef Kafta:**
- 1.5 lbs ground beef (or lamb, or a mix)
- 1/2 onion, finely minced
- 1/2 cup fresh parsley, minced
- Spice mix (above)

1. Mix ground beef with onion, parsley, and spice mix. Form into oval patties.
2. Optional: sear patties in a hot skillet 1 min per side to seal (not required).
3. Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly oil a large baking tray.
4. Layer: potato rounds, kafta patties, tomato slices, onion rings.
5. Cover with foil. Bake 40 min.
6. Remove foil, bake 15–20 more min until potatoes are cooked through and meat is browned.
7. Drizzle with tahini sauce and sprinkle parsley.
