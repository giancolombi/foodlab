# Egg Muffins

**Freezer-friendly:** Yes | **Prep:** 10 min | **Cook:** 20 min | **Makes:** 12 muffins

## Ingredients
- 10 large eggs
- 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or pepper jack)
- 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
- 1 bell pepper, finely diced
- 1/2 onion, finely diced
- Salt, pepper, garlic powder to taste
- Cooking spray or olive oil for muffin tin

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Grease a 12-cup muffin tin.
2. Whisk eggs with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
3. Divide spinach, bell pepper, and onion evenly among muffin cups.
4. Pour egg mixture over veggies, filling each cup about 3/4 full.
5. Top with shredded cheese.
6. Bake 18–20 min until eggs are set and tops are lightly golden.
7. Let cool 5 min before removing from tin.

## Freezing
- Cool completely. Place in a single layer in a freezer bag.
- Freeze up to 3 months.
- Reheat: microwave 60–90 seconds from frozen.
